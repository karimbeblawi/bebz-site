Refund Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee. If you are not satisfied with BebzTV for any reason, contact us within 30 days of your purchase and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

1. Free Trial

BebzTV offers a free 30-day trial with no credit card required. We encourage you to fully evaluate the app during this period before subscribing.

2. 30-Day Refund Guarantee

If you purchase a subscription and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 30 days of the original payment date. No reason is required. To request a refund, email us at support@bebz.tv with your order details.

Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

3. Annual Subscriptions

Annual subscriptions are billed once per year. You may cancel at any time to prevent future renewals. If you cancel within 30 days of a renewal charge, we will refund that renewal in full.

4. Lifetime Subscriptions

Lifetime subscriptions are a one-time purchase. If you are unsatisfied, you may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

5. Renewal Reminders

We send an email reminder 7 days before any annual subscription renews so you are never surprised by a charge.

6. How to Request a Refund

Email support@bebz.tv with your Device ID, email used for payment, and purchase date. We respond to all refund requests within 24 hours.

7. Contact

Questions? Use our contact form or email support@bebz.tv.
