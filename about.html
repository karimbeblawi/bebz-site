BebzTV is an independent media player app for Roku, built by a small team passionate about beautiful, fast, and reliable software.

Our Mission

To build the most elegant and reliable media player experience on Roku — fast, intuitive, and accessible to everyone including Arabic-speaking audiences.

🛡️

What We Are

BebzTV is a software application only. We do not provide, host, sell, or distribute any media content. Users connect their own personal media subscriptions.

🌍

Where We Are

BebzTV is based in Montreal, Canada. We are an independent software company committed to quality and customer satisfaction.

💬

Support

We respond to all support requests within 24 hours. Reach us at support@bebz.tv or via our contact form.

Important Notice

BebzTV is a media player application. We do not provide any video content, live channels, movies, or series. All media is sourced entirely by the user through their own third-party subscriptions.

Users are solely responsible for ensuring that the content they access is legally licensed in their region. BebzTV does not condone or facilitate access to unlicensed content.
